Read me first 💛

Your money data lives on THIS device. Only here.
I never see it, I can't recover it, and it won't sync between your devices: whichever phone, tablet, or computer you set up on is where your books live. That's the honest tradeoff for real privacy.

Two rules that will save your life:

1. Back up regularly. Settings → Back Up Now saves a file you keep. If you clear your browser, switch to a new device, or forget your PIN, that backup is the ONLY way to get your numbers back.

2. If you set a PIN, don't lose it. A PIN is optional, but once set your data is encrypted with it. No PIN, no way in, not even for me. Lost PIN = restore from your backup file.
